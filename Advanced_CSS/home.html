Welcome to Orange Beach, Alabama

Discover the Gem of the Gulf Coast

Introduction

Nestled along Alabama's stunning Gulf Coast, Orange Beach is a premier destination for relaxation, adventure, and natural beauty. With its sugar-white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant local culture, Orange Beach offers something for everyone, from families to couples seeking a romantic getaway.

Dining and Nightlife

Things to do at Orange Beach

  • Water Adventures: Take a dolphin cruise, go deep-sea fishing, or charter a boat for a day of adventure on the water.
  • Local Attractions: Explore the Wharf, featuring shopping, dining, and entertainment options, including a ferris wheel and live music.
  • Dining: Savor fresh seafood at local restaurants, offering a taste of the Gulf with stunning waterfront views.
