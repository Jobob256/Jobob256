The Tourist Attractions of Orange Beach

Gulf State Park

This expansive park features beautiful beaches, nature trails, and a fishing pier. Visitors can enjoy hiking, biking, and wildlife viewing, along with access to pristine stretches of sand along the Gulf of Mexico.

Waterfront Park

A family-friendly destination with picnic areas, playgrounds, and a fishing pier. The park offers scenic views of the water and is a great spot for leisurely walks and outdoor activities.

The Wharf

A lively entertainment district with shops, restaurants, and a marina. The Wharf hosts seasonal events and concerts, and features an amphitheater, a Ferris wheel, and a variety of dining options, making it a hub of activity.

Adventure Island

Perfect for families, Adventure Island is an amusement park featuring go-karts, mini-golf, bumper boats, and arcade games. Spend a fun-filled day enjoying the attractions and creating lasting memories with loved ones.
